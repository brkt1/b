Our Approach

Comprehensive Business Development Services

The aim of our BDS support, coaching and training is to enhance entrepreneurial competencies of business owners, start-ups, and SMEs to enhance employment opportunities. Our BDS package comprises of six components starting from business idea generation and business plan development to access to finance linkage.

Through our digitalized BDS provision, we avail affordable, tailored, and end-to-end business advisory support to young entrepreneurs. Child Safeguards Policy and BDS Guidelines are strictly applicable in our BDS programming.

Advocacy

Enhancing Ethiopia's Entrepreneurial Ecosystem

Policy Advocacy & Stakeholder Engagement

At Biruhminds Consult, we are dedicated to fostering economic resilience and growth, particularly through our focus on Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). We recognize the multifaceted challenges, from limited access to finance to navigating regulatory complexities and responding to dynamic market demands.

Our flagship "Liyu Milketa" initiative serves as a platform to strengthen SMEs by enhancing access to finance and facilitating high-quality Business Development Services (BDS). This program advocates for policy changes, addresses institutional challenges, and fosters partnerships among key stakeholders.

Ethiopia's SME Landscape

In the diverse fabric of Ethiopia's economic landscape, the intricate threads of Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) weave a narrative of resilience, innovation, and untapped potential. The country's entrepreneurial spirit is embodied by the countless individuals who embark on the journey of creating and sustaining SMEs.

These enterprises, ranging from startups to established businesses, play a pivotal role in driving economic vibrancy and fostering local empowerment. Our engagement in SME development goes beyond conventional approaches - we believe in empowering individuals, fostering strategic thinking, and creating an environment where SMEs can thrive, even in challenging circumstances.
